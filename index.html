Work

Shadow Careers provide an accelerated, effective and efficient way for students to become economically independent and contributors to the SA economy. By creating permanent careers they are not only changing the students lives but that of their families, their community and our country. They place focus on unemployed school leavers (matriculants) and excluded youth. Their students are taken through a three-month purpose specific programme that, upon successful completion, concludes with a career offer. They only recruit the number of students that they are able to place into careers, thereby helping to secure them employment. Their candidates are matched to the hiring company’s recruitment criteria to ensure seamless transition and successful outcomes - promoting employee retention.
